Forecasting Google stock prices

The stock market

"Those who leave early are saved, but the ball is so splendid no one wants to leave while there is still time. So everybody keeps asking - what time is it? But none of the clocks have hands." -George Goodman.

The stock market is a considered a risky method of investment one could could get caught up in winnings and lose it all, like gambling, but someone there are patterns that can be found is much large amounts of data this is hard for a single person to assess over that makes it hard to be sure of a trade. That is why the stock market is adapting a new method of trading using the powerful capabilities of Machine learning (ML). I replicated a project that can find patterns in such large amounts of data that it could analyze and predict future google stock proses with a accuracy rate of around 80%!!!!

The program

Using the tesorflow framwork I was able to pridict the 2018 stock price year on a month by month forcast. This project was based on .csv files on the hight, low, opening and closing data of Googles stock price 3 years prior. Based on this data set and the ML program the ~80% accuracy was vary good.
